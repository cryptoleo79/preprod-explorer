How DUST Generation Works

DUST is the gas token on the Midnight Network, required for all transaction fees. It is generated passively by holders of NIGHT tokens on the Cardano blockchain.

  • Each NIGHT token generates DUST at a fixed rate of 8,267 specks/star/sec
  • Maximum DUST capacity is 5 DUST per NIGHT held
  • Full capacity is reached in approximately ~7 days
  • DUST is non-transferable and decays when backing NIGHT is spent
  • You must register your reward address at the Nethermind DUST Generator

Use this calculator to estimate how much DUST you would generate for any NIGHT amount. The actual rate is determined on-chain and may vary slightly.
